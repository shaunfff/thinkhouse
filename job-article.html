Are you a client service wiz bursting with creativity but also exceptionally organised?
Are you one of those people who can get the best out of people and knows how to build lasting client relationships? If you are, we'd love to meet you. We're looking for an Account Manager / Senior Account Manager to join our client service team

Approach

We're looking for a super organised and experienced Account Manager / Senior Account Manager to join our client service team and take the reins on an amazing portfolio of accounts. You'll have full ownership for a number of major FMCG and consumer facing brands and you'll get to work across a broad range of channels and service offerings from Thinkhouse including; Brand Strategy, Social Media, PR, Creative - including OOH & TV.

What you'll be doing:

Leading the key client relationships and day to day management of account portfolio.

Building strong relationships with clients, to unlock account growth opportunities and retain business.

Leading client response to briefs in conjunction with service teams - Planning & Strategy, Social, PR, Film, Photography, Events etc.

Creation of scope of work and estimates in line with client budgets and monitoring accounts to ensure no over-servicing takes place.

Be the brand guardian and the voice of the client internally, ensuring any plans or activity always goes back to the client brief and job to be done.

Meet and exceed all KPI's and financial targets for account portfolio

Experience & Skills:

A team player, willing to go the extra mile for your clients and colleagues

Agency experience working as an Account Manager / Senior Account Manager, with proven success managing and growing accounts.

Ability to take initiative and make decisions based on business principles

Education:

Educated to degree level or similar

Previous agency experience is a must
